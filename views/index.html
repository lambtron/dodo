Automatically unfollow Twitter users in a private Twitter list each month.

Getting Started

1. Create a (private) list on Twitter with 'Dodo' in its title
2. Authorize your Twitter account below (so Dodo has permission!)
3. Follow and add Twitter users to your Dodo list
4. Every month, this app will automatically unfollow them for you

Other uses

1. A ~/.js script that automatically adds and mutes accounts you follow to your Dodo list (script is here)
2. Trapper, a service that automatically follows back users who have followed you throughout the day (runs daily)
3. Our HTTP Dodo-API that mutes and adds an account to your Dodo list
4. Hunter, a service that listens to the firehose of tweets and follows/mutes/adds to dodo list the tweeter
